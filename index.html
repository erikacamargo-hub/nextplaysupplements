About Us

Next Play Supplements was founded by Erika and Matt, a husband-and-wife team united by a shared belief in wellness, balance, and intentional living.

As a Brazilian woman and mother of two, Erika experienced firsthand the challenges of maintaining energy, focus, and self-care within the demands of everyday life. She was searching for products that could support her routine in a practical way — without overstimulation or energy crashes.

Matt, with years of experience in the retail supplement industry, became not only a supporter of Erika’s vision, but a key partner in bringing it to life.

Together, they created Next Play Supplements with the mission of developing thoughtfully formulated products that integrate seamlessly into daily routines — combining performance with balance and care.

Their journey is rooted in partnership, purpose, and the belief that feeling well every day should be accessible, intentional, and sustainable.
